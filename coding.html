During my time at Binghamton I discovered an interest in coding with HTML and CSS. My code was written using Sublime and then made public online via GitHub. Here are a couple interactive websites I have designed and coded myself.

'Let's Go Camping' is a bitmap styled, choose your own adventure game. The player makes choices about their camping trip with the goal of making it through the night. Given the goal of the site, there are many HTML pages connected in a web of sorts that ultimately bring the player full circle back to the home page. This was my first big project with HTML and CSS where I was able to decide my own content and have my own style choices. The bitmap style gives the game a nostalgic feel and leaves room for later additions in the future. Click here or click the gif below to play!

'The Quaking Aspen' site was a bit of a passion project for me. The given prompt for this assignment was to create a site for any person, place or thing that isn't readily found on the web. I decided to focus my site on my favorite tree, the quaking aspen. The site invites the viewer to explore and interact with various elements to learn about the tree. I familiarized myself with map tags and hidden elements throughout the code. Click here or click the image of the tree on the right to explore the site!
