This poster was designed for my Graphic Design III course at Binghamton University. For this project, the goal was essentially to digitize an image that previously only existed as print. I also chose to adjust the layout slightly to make it a more traditional, easy to read poster layout. To recreate this image, I worked primarily in Adobe Illustrator and familiarized myslef with grid tools.
