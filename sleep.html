"Sleep Through the Ages" was created for my Graphic Design III course. I wanted to explore how sleep patterns varied across different life stages. Simple icons were designed to show the various age groups in an easy to undersstand and light-hearted way. I kept my color palette warm and representative of sunrise and sunset. Ultimately, I feel that my approach to this project led to an informative and aesthetically pleasing poster design.
